Sine Law and Cosine Law


Not all triangles are right triangles.


Luckily, the Sine and Cosine Laws are formulas used in Trigonometry
to find the length of the sides and angles in an oblique triangle.

Sine Law


Sine Law is used to determine the missing side lengths or angles in oblique triangles.
According to the rule, we must know the length of a side and
the measurement of its opposite angle in order to use its formula.
